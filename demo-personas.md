## Usage Guidelines for Facilitators

**Session 1: Priority Building**
- Have participants choose ONE persona 
- Use that persona's background to create realistic FY26 priorities
- Encourage specific details (project names, metrics, timelines)
- Validate that priorities align with persona's role and goals

**Session 2: Interview Prep**  
- Participants can use same persona OR choose different one
- Provide both job descriptions or let them bring real ones
- Focus on strategic positioning rather than perfect fit
- Emphasize pattern application over persona perfection

**Benefits of Using Personas:**
- ✅ No confidential information concerns
- ✅ Consistent baseline for all participants  
- ✅ Can share and compare outputs freely
- ✅ Realistic complexity without personal stakes
- ✅ Reusable for future workshop sessions
