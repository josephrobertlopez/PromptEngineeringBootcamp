- **Instructions**: Participants pick different persona than demo

### **Minute-by-Minute Facilitation**:
```

32:00 - Quick debrief: "How many got output they'd submit to manager?"
33:00 - "Most struggled with specificity - let's try systematic approach"
35:00 - Transition: "Now use Priority Builder methodology"
```

### **Coaching Notes**:
- Don't help too much - let them feel the pain
- Take mental notes of struggles for later discussion
- Watch for generic vs specific outputs
