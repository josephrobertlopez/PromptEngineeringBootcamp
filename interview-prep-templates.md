# Interview Preparation 4-File Workflow

## File 1: knowledge-base.md

### Interview Fundamentals

#### STAR Method Framework
- **Situation**: Context and background  
- **Task**: What needed to be accomplished
- **Action**: Steps you took (focus on YOUR actions)
- **Result**: Outcome and impact (quantified when possible)

#### Common Senior Manager Questions
1. "Tell me about a time you led a complex project"
2. "How do you handle competing stakeholder priorities?"
3. "Describe your approach to building and developing teams"
4. "Give an example of how you drove strategic change"
5. "What's your experience with digital/AI transformation?"
6. "How do you measure success in your role?"
7. "Tell me about a difficult client situation you handled"
8. "Describe a time when you had to influence without authority"

#### Positioning Strategy Options
- **Technical Expert**: Emphasize deep functional skills and specialized knowledge
- **Strategic Leader**: Focus on business impact, vision, and change management
- **Balanced Bridge**: Demonstrate both technical depth and business acumen
- **Innovation Driver**: Highlight transformation and forward-thinking approaches

#### Interview Success Metrics
- Clear compelling narrative connecting past experience → role requirements
- 3-5 strong STAR examples covering different competencies
- Questions prepared that show strategic thinking
- Authentic confidence in chosen positioning
