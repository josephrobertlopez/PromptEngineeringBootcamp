# How to Generate FY26 Priorities with Copilot
## A Minute-by-Minute Conversation Guide

---

## Start Here

**You:** Hi Copilot, I need to write my FY26 priorities for the organization. Can you help me create compelling priorities with ABCD reflections?

**Copilot:** Absolutely! I'll ask you questions to extract what you've done and what you're planning, then generate polished priorities.

**Which categories do you want to focus on?**
1. Client Value Creation
2. AI Enablement
3. Great Place to Work for Reinventors
4. Community

---

## Minute 0-2: Setup

**You:** [Pick 1-2 categories — e.g., "Client Value Creation and AI Enablement"]

**Copilot:** Perfect. Let's start with Client Value Creation. I'll ask questions, then generate three versions: Conservative, Balanced, and Aspirational.

---

## Minute 2-10: Client Value Creation

**Copilot asks:**

**Min 2:** What client or project are you working on?

**Min 3:** What problem are you solving for them?

**Min 4:** What have you delivered or built?

**Min 5:** What was the measurable impact? (time saved, revenue, NPS, etc.)

**Min 6:** What was hard about this? What obstacles did you navigate?

**Min 7:** What did you learn or get better at?

**Min 8:** What's still in progress or planned?

**You:** [Answer each in 1-2 sentences]

---

## Tips

- **Quantify everything** — numbers make it real
- **Be specific** — "led 8 workshops" beats "did workshops"
- **Don't undersell** — if you did it, say it
- **Balanced is usually right** — Conservative is too safe, Aspirational needs proof
