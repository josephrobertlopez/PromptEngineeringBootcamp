- Present AI strategy and progress to C-level executives and board members

### Required Qualifications
- 8+ years experience in strategy consulting, digital transformation, or AI implementation
- 3+ years in financial services or highly regulated industry
- Proven track record managing $2M+ technology initiatives
- Experience leading teams of 10+ professionals across multiple business units
- Strong presentation skills and executive presence

### Preferred Qualifications

- Previous consulting experience (McKinsey, BCG, Bain, the organization, Deloitte)
- Published thought leadership in AI/digital transformation

### Success Metrics
- Deliver 3+ production AI solutions within first 18 months
- Achieve 15% operational efficiency improvement through AI implementation
- Build AI center of excellence with 25+ trained professionals
- Establish thought leadership position in industry AI applications

## SELECTION GUIDE

**For Interview Exercise:**

1. **Choose based on your background alignment** - pick the role closest to your experience for realistic practice

2. **Consider career goals** - select the type of role you're actually targeting for authentic positioning

3. **Difficulty levels**:
   - **Easier**: Roles aligned with your current function
   - **Moderate**: Roles in same industry but different function  
   - **Challenging**: Different industry and function (stretch positioning)

4. **Exercise focus**:
   - Practice **ReAct pattern**: Analyze requirements → Map experience → Identify positioning strategy
   - Apply **Tree of Thoughts**: Generate multiple positioning approaches → Evaluate → Choose best
   - Create **systematic materials**: Positioning statement + STAR examples + strategic questions

**All job descriptions designed for realistic interview preparation using systematic prompt engineering methodology.**
